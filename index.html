Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of businesses is PropoKit for?

PropoKit is ideal for any business that sells products or services and needs to create itemized, visual proposals. It's especially popular with home automation companies, AV installers, creative agencies, consultants, and contractors.

How does the AI feature work?

Our AI, powered by Google's Gemini, is integrated directly into your product library. When you add a new item, you just provide the name and model. The AI then instantly generates a high-quality image, a detailed description, and a technical scope of work, saving you immense time.

What are AI Credits and how do they work?

An AI Credit is used each time you generate the full set of details (image, description, etc.) for one product. The Pro+ AI plan includes 200 credits per month, which is enough for most businesses to build their core library and add new products. If you need more, you can easily purchase additional credit packs.

What happens after my 14-day free trial?

After your trial ends, you can choose to upgrade to one of our paid plans to keep access to all the features. If you choose not to, your account will be moved to our free Starter plan. Your proposals and data will remain safe.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no long-term contracts or hidden fees. You will retain access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Your proposals and customer information are protected with the same security standards that banks use. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I use my own branding and templates?

Yes! PropoKit is designed to be fully customizable. You can upload your logo, set your brand colors, choose from our professional templates, or create completely custom designs. Every proposal will reflect your unique brand identity.

Do you offer customer support?

We provide comprehensive support including live chat, email support, video tutorials, and a detailed help center. Our support team is available 24/7 to help you get the most out of PropoKit and ensure your success.
